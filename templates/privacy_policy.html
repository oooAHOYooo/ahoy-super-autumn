Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you:

  • Subscribe to our newsletter
  • RSVP for events
  • Submit artist performance proposals
  • Contact us through our website
  • Use our services

Types of Information

The information we may collect includes:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number
  • Event Information: RSVP details, guest count, event preferences
  • Artist Submissions: Performance proposals, availability, portfolio links
  • Communication Data: Messages you send to us
  • Newsletter Data: Email address, signup date, device type, referrer domain, regional information
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics about how you use our website (see Analytics section below)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Send you newsletters and event updates
  • Process RSVPs and manage events
  • Review and respond to artist performance proposals
  • Respond to your inquiries and requests
  • Analyze website usage and improve user experience
  • Understand our audience demographics and preferences
  • Prevent spam and protect against security threats
  • Comply with legal obligations

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share information with trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website and conducting our business
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information when required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred

Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Security Measures Include:

  • CSRF Protection: Cross-Site Request Forgery tokens on all forms
  • Rate Limiting: Protection against spam and DoS attacks
  • Input Validation: All user inputs are sanitized and validated
  • Content Filtering: Automatic spam detection and filtering
  • Security Headers: Content Security Policy, X-Frame-Options, and more
  • Session Management: Secure session handling for admin access
  • Data Encryption: Sensitive data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Password-protected admin areas
  • Regular Security Updates: Keeping all dependencies current

Analytics and Privacy-First Tracking

We use a privacy-focused analytics system that respects your privacy while helping us understand how our website is used. Unlike traditional analytics services, we do not use third-party tracking or cookies.

Our Analytics Approach:

  • No Third-Party Tracking: We don't use Google Analytics or other external tracking services
  • Anonymous Data Only: All visitor data is anonymized using secure hashing
  • No Personal Information: We never collect names, emails, or personal details in our analytics
  • Local Storage Only: All analytics data is stored locally on our servers
  • No Cross-Site Tracking: We don't track you across other websites
  • No Data Selling: We never sell or share analytics data with third parties

What We Track (Anonymously):

  • Page Views: Which pages are visited most
  • Device Information: Mobile, tablet, or desktop usage
  • Browser Data: Browser type and version for compatibility
  • Traffic Sources: How visitors find our site (Google, social media, direct)
  • Time Patterns: Peak visiting hours and daily patterns
  • Geographic Region: General location (country/region level only)

What We DON'T Track:

  • Your IP address (only used for anonymization)
  • Personal identification information
  • Individual browsing behavior across sessions
  • Data that could identify you personally

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your personal information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Data portability
  • Request analytics data deletion (though it's already anonymized)
  • Opt-out of analytics tracking (contact us to request this)

To exercise these rights, please contact us at hello@ahoyindie.com. We will respond to all requests within 30 days.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Retention Periods:

  • Newsletter Subscriptions: Until you unsubscribe
  • Event RSVPs: Until the event is completed plus 1 year
  • Artist Submissions: Until you request deletion or 2 years after submission
  • Contact Information: Until you request deletion
  • Analytics Data: Stored locally for up to 2 years, then automatically deleted
  • Security Logs: Retained for 90 days for security monitoring

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

International Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this privacy policy.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of our services after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Our Privacy Commitment

At AHOY Indie Media, we believe privacy is a fundamental right. We've built our platform with privacy-first principles:

Privacy-First Features We've Implemented:

  • Self-Hosted Analytics: We built our own analytics system instead of using Google Analytics or other third-party trackers
  • No Tracking Cookies: We don't use tracking cookies or pixel tags
  • Minimal Data Collection: We only collect what's necessary to provide our services
  • Local Data Storage: All data stays on our servers - we don't share with third parties
  • Anonymized Analytics: Visitor data is hashed and anonymized before storage
  • Rate Limiting: We protect against spam and abuse without collecting personal data
  • Secure Forms: All forms use CSRF protection and input validation
  • No Data Selling: We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information

Why This Matters:

Traditional analytics services like Google Analytics collect extensive personal data and track users across the web. We chose a different approach - one that respects your privacy while still helping us understand how to improve our platform for the New Haven arts community.

Our analytics help us answer questions like "Which events are most popular?" and "When do people visit our site?" without knowing who you are or tracking you elsewhere on the internet.
